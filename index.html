What we do

Preworkout is a popular tool for getting an edge. This site was developed to give athletes the tools needed to make their own blends, educate themselves, and hone their craft.

This site was launched to address the lack of clear nutritional information disguised as a "proprietary blend". A supplement blend can be 50% ingredient A, 40% ingredient B, 10% ingredient C, and can be advertised and labeled the same as if it were 90% A, 5% ingredient B, 5% ingredient C. "But some supplements are more potent than others." True! But the company doesn't have to provide that information, nor do they even have to provide an efficacious dose.

It's difficult enough staying on top of who makes what, vendors, sources, and updates to blends. An unregulated market makes it more difficult. When a company obfuscates nutritional information, it only harms the athlete. Educate yourself, do your thing, and be about it.
